ABOUT

MVE (Monk Vision Entertainment) is a music collective with 9 members, with 6 being music and content creators. TyThaMonk the founder of the group founded it in late 2017 where MVE was solely mean for YouTube content then slowly transitioned into a group full of amazing artists and creators in general.
